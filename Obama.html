Barack Obama and Hillary Clinton

Barack Obama

Barack Obama served as the 44th President of the United States from January 20, 2009, to January 20, 2017. He was the first African American president and was known for his policies on healthcare reform, the economic stimulus package, and foreign diplomacy. Obama was born on August 4, 1961, in Honolulu, Hawaii. Before his presidency, he was a U.S. Senator from Illinois.

Hillary Clinton

Hillary Clinton is an American politician and diplomat who served as the First Lady of the United States from 1993 to 2001, U.S. Senator from New York from 2001 to 2009, and Secretary of State from 2009 to 2013. She was the Democratic Party's nominee for President in the 2016 election, becoming the first woman to be nominated for president by a major party. Clinton was born on October 26, 1947, in Chicago, Illinois.
